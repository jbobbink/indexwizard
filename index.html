Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

This tool is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data:

No data shared with my server

I do not collect, store, or share any of your data on my servers. Your URLs, search console data, and inspection results are never transmitted to or stored on our infrastructure.

Direct communication with Google

All data requests are made directly between your browser, Google's API client library (api.js) and Google's Search Console API. When you authenticate, you grant permission for this application to access your Google Search Console data on your behalf. This communication happens directly with Google - there is no server involved.

Authentication

The webapp (app.js) uses Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication via Google Identity Services, running entirely in your browser. Your Google credentials are never shared with us. The authentication tokens are stored only in your browser's memory and used solely to make requests directly to Google's API.

Fully client-Side

This application runs entirely in your browser. There is no backend server processing your data. All API calls go directly from your browser to Google's servers.

Local processing

All data processing, including displaying results and generating exports (CSV/JSON), happens entirely in your browser. Your data stays on your device.

No tracking

I use Vercel Web Analytics to understand aggregated usage of my website. It does not use cookies and stores only anonymized, aggregated data. I do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. I do not track your usage patterns or behavior.
